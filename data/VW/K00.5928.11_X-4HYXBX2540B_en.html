Removing and installing turbocharger

Special tools and workshop equipment required
  • Hose clamps, up to Ø 25 mm 3094
  • Engine bung set VAS 6122
If a mechanical fault is discovered on the turbocharger (e.g. a destroyed compressor impeller), it is not sufficient to just renew the turbocharger. To avoid any subsequent damage, the following work must be carried out:
  • Check air filter housing, air filter element and air inlet hoses for contamination.
  • Check the whole charge air path and charge air cooler for foreign bodies.
  • If foreign bodies are discovered in the charge air system, clean the charge air path and, if necessary, renew the charge air cooler.
Removing
  • Observe rules for cleanliness Chapter“Rules for cleanliness”.
Note
  • Possible causes of mechanical failure of the turbocharger may be foreign material from the exhaust manifold.
  • Remove emission control module Chapter“Removing and installing emission control module”.
  • Remove right engine cover panel Chapter“Removing and installing engine cover”.
  • Remove front upper coolant pipe for turbocharger Chapter“Removing and installing coolant pipes”.
  • Unscrew bolt 1 and push exhaust gas recirculation pipe 2 backwards slightly.
  • Unscrew bolt 4 and pull off coolant pipe 5 from turbocharger.
  • Unscrew bolts 1, pull off air pipe 2 from turbocharger but leave in installation position.
  • Unscrew exhaust gas temperature sender 1 G235 1 on turbocharger.
  • Release screw-type clips 1, 2 and push onto exhaust manifold / turbocharger.
  • Unscrew bolts arrows, place automatic glow period control unit J179 with bracket to one side.
  • Unscrew nuts arrows for exhaust gas recirculation pipe.
  • Disconnect connector 1 for charge pressure control module GX34.
  • Remove bolts 5 and 4.
  • Unscrew nuts 2, 3. To do this, push actuator rod for charge pressure control module GX34 upwards.
  • Pull out turbocharger 1 with exhaust gas recirculation pipe 2 towards rear slightly in direction of arrow.
  • Unscrew bolts 3 and remove turbocharger 1.
Installing
Install in reverse order of removal, observing the following:
Note
  • Renew seals, nuts and O-ring after removal.
  • Secure all hose connections with correct type of hose clips (as original equipment) Electronic parts catalogue.
  • Fill turbocharger with engine oil at oil supply line connection.
  • After installing turbocharger, run engine for about 1 minute at idling speed to ensure that oil is supplied to turbocharger; do not rev up immediately.
  • Install rear coolant pipe for turbocharger and front upper coolant pipe for turbocharger Chapter“Removing and installing coolant pipes”.
If turbocharger was renewed, adaption routine must be performed after installation
  • Connect vehicle diagnostic tester.
  • 0001 - Engine electronics, functions
  • 0001 - Work steps after component replacement
  • In engine control unit, clear any event entries which may have been stored during procedure Vehicle diagnostic tester.
Note
  • Secure all hose connections with correct type of hose clips (as original equipment) Electronic parts catalogue.
  • Install right engine cover panel Chapter“Removing and installing engine cover”.
  • Remove emission control module Chapter“Removing and installing emission control module”.
