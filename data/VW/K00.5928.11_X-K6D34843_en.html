Removing cylinder head, bank 1 (right side)

Special tools and workshop equipment required
  • Hose clip pliers VAS 6362
Sequence of operations
  • Drain coolant Chapter“Draining and filling cooling system”.
  • Remove camshaft timing chain from camshaft chain sprockets Chapter“Removing camshaft timing chain from camshafts”.
  • Remove right camshaft Chapter“Removing and installing camshaft”.
Note
  • If the bolt arrow is unscrewed, the backlash must be re-adjusted Chapter“Installing right intermediate drive, cylinder bank 1 and adjusting the backlash”.
  • Unscrew bolt only when replacing the cylinder head and the intermediate drive is to be refitted to the new cylinder head.
  • Unscrew front coolant pipe and pull off from map-controlled engine cooling system thermostat F265 Chapter“Removing and installing coolant pipes”.
  • Unscrew bolts arrows and push coolant connection 1 to one side.
  • Unscrew bolts arrows and push upper right coolant pipe 1 to one side
  • Unscrew bolt 2 of retaining clip for high-pressure line.
  • Unscrew guide pin 1.
  • Attach fixing T40309/2 with fitted bolt T40309/3 to cylinder head as shown in illustration.
  • Support tensioning rail. To do this, screw in knurled screw 3 of bracket T40309/1 until fixing T40309/2 is in contact with tensioning rail.
  • Release guide pin 2 and unscrew by hand.
Note
  • For avoiding damage to the thread in the cylinder head, unscrewing the guide pin for the tensioning rail must be easy. If necessary, turn the knurled screw 3 further slightly.
  • Loosen knurled screw 3, unscrew fitted bolt T40309/3, remove fixing T40309/2.
  • Loosen cylinder head bolts in the sequence 1 … 8.
  • Unscrew bolts and carefully remove cylinder head.
  • Place cylinder head onto soft surface (foam plastic).
Note
  • Seal open channels of intake and exhaust system with clean cloths or with thoroughly cleaned plugs from engine bung set VAS 6122.
